BROWSER_NOT_SUPPORTED

• BROWSER_NOT_SUPPORTED: = "BROWSER_NOT_SUPPORTED"

Browser is not supported by Rokt solution

GENERAL_ERROR

• GENERAL_ERROR: = "GENERAL_ERROR"

General error

NO_OFFERS

• NO_OFFERS: = "NO_OFFERS"

No offers available for any placement on a page.

Depending on a partner's use case lack of offers may or may not be considered an error. In essence, some partners may display placements that are integral part of a displayed page. In the following cases lack of offers hinders the main purpose of the page and partner may choose to gracefully handle the situation, for example by moving to the next page in a user journey. For most of the partners though the following error could be safely disregarded as widget will gracefully handle lack of offers by either not showing placement or displaying a pre-configured fallback message.

NO_PLACEMENTS

• NO_PLACEMENTS: = "NO_PLACEMENTS"

No placements available for a page.

Depending on a partner's use case lack of placements may or may not be considered an error. In essence, some partners may display placements that are integral part of a displayed page. In the following cases not showing a placements hinders the main purpose of the page and partner may choose to gracefully handle the situation, for example by moving to the next page in a user journey. For most of the partners though the following error could be safely disregarded as there may be valid reasons why placements are not displayed, for example: tag being used as a conversion tag, placement has reached a frequency cap etc.

PLACEMENTS_RESPONSE

PLACEMENT_NOT_ATTACHED

• PLACEMENT_NOT_ATTACHED: = "PLACEMENT_NOT_ATTACHED"

Raised when Rokt could not attach placement to element.

There could be a number of reasons for it:

  • target selector was not provided
  • target selector did not find any matching element
  • error thrown

In the case of exception being thrown it may relate to selector starting with a number or containing special characters that require escaping. Please follow examples that can be found here: https://mathiasbynens.be/notes/css-escapes
